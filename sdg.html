Sustainable Development Goal 14: Life Below Water

Healthy oceans and seas are essential to our existence.They cover 70 percent of our planet and we rely on them for food, energy and water. Yet, we have managed to do tremendous damage to these precious resources. We must protect them by eliminating pollution and overfishing and immediately start to responsibly manage and protect all marine life around the world.



The Following are the goals related to sdg-14 Life Under Water

  • Secure Marine Life. The main goal for life under water is to secure marine life in the upcoming years.
  • Overfishing. To avoid overfishing there should be a rule made by the goverment.
  • Ocean Acidification. To avoid the acidification of ocean we should minimize the chemical waste coming out from the industries.
